# MCP4 Compliance Documentation Guide

## Overview

MCP4 maintains comprehensive compliance documentation aligned with international standards for information security, privacy, cloud services, and AI management.

## ISO Standards Coverage

### ISO 27001 - Information Security Management System (ISMS)
Establishes the foundation for protecting information assets across all MCP4 systems and services.

### ISO 27701 - Privacy Information Management System (PIMS)
Extends ISO 27001 with specific privacy controls for GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations.

### ISO 27018 - Cloud Privacy for SaaS
Addresses cloud-specific privacy considerations, including multi-tenancy and data location.

### ISO 42001 - AI Management System (AIMS)
Establishes ethical AI practices, risk management, and transparency requirements.

## Policy Structure

All policies are accessible at `/docs/policy` and include:

- **Purpose**: Why the policy exists
- **Scope**: Who and what it applies to
- **Sections**: Detailed requirements and procedures
- **Metadata**: Version, owner, review cycle, effective date

## Key Policies

### Security (ISO 27001)
- **Information Security Policy**: Overall security framework
- **Access Control Policy**: User roles, authentication, authorization
- Additional policies for encryption, backups, incident response

### Privacy (ISO 27701)
- **Privacy Policy**: Public-facing data protection commitments
- **Data Protection Policy**: Internal privacy controls and procedures
- Additional policies for cookies, data subject rights, retention

### Cloud (ISO 27018)
- **Cloud Data Protection Policy**: Cloud-specific security controls
- Sub-processor management, data location, multi-tenancy

### AI (ISO 42001)
- **AI Ethics & Transparency Policy**: Ethical AI principles
- **AI Risk Management Policy**: AI-specific risk assessment and mitigation
- Model governance, bias prevention, safety controls

## Policy Management

### Version Control
- All policies version-controlled in Git
- Changes tracked with commit history
- Previous versions accessible through Git history

### Review Cycle
- **Annual**: Most security and privacy policies
- **Semi-Annual**: AI-related policies due to rapid evolution
- **As Needed**: When regulations change or incidents occur

### Approval Process
1. Policy draft created by responsible team
2. Legal and compliance review
3. Management approval
4. Communication to relevant stakeholders
5. Publication on website
