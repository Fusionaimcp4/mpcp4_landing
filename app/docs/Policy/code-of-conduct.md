# Code of Conduct & General Compliance Policy  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Owner:** Chief Executive Officer (CEO) / Compliance Officer  
**Effective Date:** 2025-11-05  
**Review Cycle:** Annual or upon legal or organizational change  

---

## 1. Purpose
This Code of Conduct and General Compliance Policy defines the ethical, professional, and legal standards expected of everyone acting on behalf of **MCP4.ai**, including employees, contractors, advisors, and partners.  
It ensures that business is conducted with **integrity, transparency, fairness, and accountability**, and that all activities comply with applicable laws, international standards, and MCP4.ai’s internal policies.

---

## 2. Scope
Applies to all individuals representing or working with MCP4.ai and its subsidiaries or projects — **Fusion**, **Voxe**, and **NeuroSwitch** — regardless of location, role, or contractual status.

---

## 3. Core Principles
All personnel must adhere to the following principles in their daily work:

1. **Integrity and Honesty** – Act truthfully and in good faith in every interaction.  
2. **Respect and Fairness** – Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with dignity, without discrimination or harassment.  
3. **Accountability** – Accept responsibility for actions, decisions, and outcomes.  
4. **Compliance** – Follow all applicable laws, regulations, and internal policies.  
5. **Confidentiality** – Protect company and customer information at all times.  
6. **Transparency** – Communicate openly about risks, mistakes, and conflicts of interest.  
7. **Security and Privacy** – Safeguard all data per MCP4.ai’s information-security and privacy controls.  
8. **Sustainability and Ethics** – Use technology responsibly and minimize environmental impact.

---

## 4. Legal and Regulatory Compliance
MCP4.ai operates under a unified compliance program aligned with:
- **ISO 27001** – Information Security Management  
- **ISO 27701** – Privacy Information Management  
- **ISO 27018** – Cloud Data Protection  
- **ISO 42001** – Artificial Intelligence Management  
- **GDPR**, **CCPA/CPRA**, **LGPD**, **POPIA**, **FADP**, and other regional privacy laws.  

Employees must comply with all applicable international export, anti-corruption, and anti-bribery regulations (e.g., **FCPA**, **UK Bribery Act**).

---

## 5. Responsibilities
| Role | Key Responsibilities |
|------|----------------------|
| **All Personnel** | Follow this Code and report any suspected violation. |
| **Supervisors / Managers** | Model ethical conduct, support compliance training, and handle reports confidentially. |
| **Compliance Officer / DPO** | Maintain compliance frameworks, policies, and reporting mechanisms. |
| **CISO / CTO** | Ensure technical operations align with ISO security and privacy controls. |
| **CEO / Leadership Team** | Promote a culture of integrity and ensure adequate resources for compliance. |

---

## 6. Confidentiality & Data Protection
- Protect all proprietary, customer, and personal data according to the **Information Security**, **Data Protection**, and **Privacy** policies.  
- Do not share internal information without authorization.  
- Report suspected data leaks immediately to **security@mcp4.ai**.  

---

## 7. Conflicts of Interest
- Avoid activities that create or appear to create conflicts between personal interests and MCP4.ai’s business.  
- Disclose any potential conflict (financial, relational, or competitive) to the Compliance Officer before engaging.  

---

## 8. Anti-Corruption and Fair Dealing
- Bribery, facilitation payments, or unethical inducements are strictly prohibited.  
- Gifts or entertainment may only be offered or accepted if modest, transparent, and compliant with local law.  
- Deal fairly with customers, vendors, competitors, and regulators; do not misrepresent facts or manipulate results.

---

## 9. Workplace Conduct
- MCP4.ai maintains a harassment-free, inclusive, and respectful workplace.  
- Discrimination or retaliation against any individual for reporting misconduct is prohibited.  
- Bullying, hate speech, or offensive behavior (including in digital communications) is not tolerated.  

---

## 10. Information Security & Acceptable Use
- Follow all security and acceptable-use requirements defined in ISO 27001-aligned policies.  
- Use only approved tools, cloud services, and accounts for MCP4.ai business.  
- Never share passwords or authentication tokens.  
- Immediately report lost devices or suspicious activity.

---

## 11. Intellectual Property and Open-Source Compliance
- Respect third-party intellectual property rights and licensing terms.  
- Obtain approval before using or contributing to open-source software on company projects.  
- All inventions or code developed under employment or contract are owned by MCP4.ai unless otherwise agreed.

---

## 12. AI Ethics and Responsible Innovation
- AI systems must comply with the **AI Ethics & Transparency Policy** and avoid harmful, biased, or deceptive uses.  
- All model training and deployment must undergo risk and ethics review under **ISO 42001** governance.

---

## 13. Reporting Violations
- Employees are encouraged and obligated to report suspected violations of this Code or any policy.  
- Reports may be made confidentially or anonymously via:  
  - **Email:** compliance@mcp4.ai  
  - **Internal Whistleblower Form** (accessible on company intranet)  
- MCP4.ai prohibits retaliation against good-faith reporters.

---

## 14. Disciplinary Action
Violations of this Code may result in disciplinary measures up to termination, legal action, or vendor contract cancellation.  
Severity and intent are considered in determining corrective actions.

---

## 15. Training and Awareness
- All employees complete annual compliance, privacy, and information-security training.  
- Specialized teams (AI, DevOps, Legal, HR) receive additional ISO-aligned role-based training.  
- Training completion tracked in the **Compliance Training Register**.

---

## 16. Continuous Improvement
- Compliance program effectiveness reviewed quarterly by the Compliance Officer.  
- Lessons learned from audits or incidents integrated into updated procedures.  
- Employees encouraged to suggest improvements via the **Compliance Feedback Portal**.
