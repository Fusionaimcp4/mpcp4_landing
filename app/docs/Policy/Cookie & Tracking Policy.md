# Cookie & Tracking Policy  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Owner:** Data Protection Officer (DPO) / Compliance Officer  
**Effective Date:** 2025-11-05  
**Review Cycle:** Annual or upon changes to consent tooling or regulations  

---

## 1. Purpose
This Cookie & Tracking Policy explains how MCP4.ai and its products — **Fusion**, **Voxe**, and **NeuroSwitch** — use cookies and similar tracking technologies.  
It also outlines user rights, consent preferences, and data protection measures in compliance with **GDPR**, **CCPA**, **LGPD**, **POPIA**, and other privacy regulations.

---

## 2. Scope
Applies to:
- All visitors and users of **mcp4.ai**, **fusion.mcp4.ai**, and **voxe.mcp4.ai**.  
- All first-party and third-party cookies, local storage, and tracking pixels set by our websites.  
- All analytics, functional, and consent-related scripts managed through **CookieYes**.

---

## 3. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your browser or device that enable site functionality, improve performance, and collect analytical or preference data.  
MCP4.ai uses only essential and consent-based cookies necessary for secure and lawful operations.

---

## 4. Types of Cookies We Use
| Category | Purpose | Examples | Consent Required |
|-----------|----------|-----------|------------------|
| **Strictly Necessary** | Enable site functions such as login, language preference, and session management. | Session ID, CSRF token, CookieYes consent record | ❌ No |
| **Functional** | Enhance usability and remember user settings or interface preferences. | Dark-mode toggle, language selection | ✅ Yes |
| **Analytics** | Collect aggregated, anonymized usage data to improve services. | Plausible / Umami analytics events | ✅ Yes |
| **Performance** | Monitor uptime and service health across subdomains. | Ping, load-time tracking | ✅ Yes |
| **Marketing / Third-Party** | Only used if integrations require retargeting or social media embeds (currently disabled). | Facebook Pixel, Google Ads (if ever enabled) | ✅ Yes |

---

## 5. Legal Basis for Processing
| Region | Lawful Basis |
|---------|---------------|
| **EU/EEA (GDPR)** | User consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) for non-essential cookies; legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) for essential cookies. |
| **California (CCPA/CPRA)** | Opt-out of “sale or sharing” of personal data via CookieYes “Do Not Sell” link. |
| **Brazil (LGPD)** | Explicit consent for analytics and tracking cookies. |
| **South Africa (POPIA)** | Prior consent required before setting non-essential cookies. |

---

## 6. Consent Management
- MCP4.ai uses **CookieYes CMP** to collect, store, and manage cookie consent.  
- Consent banner appears automatically for visitors in regions covered by privacy regulations.  
- Users may accept, reject, or customize cookie preferences at any time.  
- The system records consent logs in the **ConsentLog** database for audit purposes, including:  
  - Consent ID  
  - Timestamp  
  - IP (anonymized)  
  - Consent categories and version  

---

## 7. Managing Cookies
Users can manage or withdraw consent at any time by:  
- Clicking **“Cookie Preferences”** in the footer of any MCP4.ai website.  
- Adjusting browser settings to delete or block cookies.  
- Opting out of analytics on a per-site basis.  

Disabling certain cookies may affect functionality or prevent login persistence.

---

## 8. Cookie Retention
| Cookie Type | Retention Period |
|--------------|------------------|
| Strictly Necessary | Session duration |
| Functional | Up to 12 months |
| Analytics | 12–24 months (aggregated and anonymized) |
| Consent Record | 12 months (for regulatory evidence) |

All retention periods comply with MCP4.ai’s **Data Retention Policy**.

---

## 9. Third-Party Services
MCP4.ai partners only with GDPR-compliant providers.  
Each service processes minimal data strictly for operational purposes:
- **CookieYes** – consent management platform (CMP)  
- **Plausible / Umami Analytics** – privacy-first, cookie-less analytics (aggregated only)  
- **Stripe** – payments and security (on Fusion/Voxe billing pages)  
- **Vercel / DigitalOcean** – hosting infrastructure (session logs only)  

No third-party advertising or tracking pixels are active on MCP4.ai domains.

---

## 10. Security Measures
- All cookies transmitted via HTTPS only.  
- Flags used: `Secure`, `HttpOnly`, and `SameSite=Strict`.  
- Cross-domain consent enabled for `*.mcp4.ai` subdomains.  
- Automated cookie scans conducted quarterly via CookieYes scanner.  

---

## 11. Your Rights
Depending on your region, you may:
- Withdraw cookie consent at any time.  
- Request access or deletion of stored consent records.  
- Object to processing based on legitimate interest.  
- File a complaint with a relevant data protection authority.  

For requests, contact **privacy@mcp4.ai**.
